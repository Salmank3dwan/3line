Our Services

At 3 Lines Restaurant, we’re more than just great food — we’re a full experience. Our services include fast and convenient dine-in, takeaway, and delivery options to suit every lifestyle. Whether you're grabbing a quick meal on the go, enjoying time with friends and family in our cozy dining space, or ordering from the comfort of your home, we’ve got you covered. We also offer catering for events and gatherings, bringing our signature flavors straight to your celebration. With a commitment to quality and speed, our goal is to serve satisfaction in every bite.

Careers

At 3 Lines Restaurant, we believe that great service starts with a passionate team. We’re always looking for enthusiastic, hardworking individuals who are eager to grow in a fast-paced and friendly environment. Whether you're starting your first job or bringing years of experience, we offer opportunities in kitchen operations, customer service, delivery, and management. Join us and become part of a team that values creativity, dedication, and the drive to deliver excellence every day. Your journey with 3 Lines could be the start of something deliciously rewarding.

Partners

At 3 Lines Restaurant, we recognize that our success is built upon strong collaborations. Over the years, we've partnered with esteemed organizations that share our commitment to quality and innovation. Notably, we are proud to be part of the GNJ Group, a diversified conglomerate that supports our growth and operational excellence. Additionally, our association with Three Lines Shipping enhances our logistics capabilities, ensuring timely delivery and efficient supply chain management across Bahrain and the GCC. These strategic partnerships enable us to consistently deliver exceptional dining experiences to our valued customers.
